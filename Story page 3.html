WHY ARE BOTSWANA ELEPHANTS SPECIAL?

Botswana has the largest elephant population on the continent due to a number of reasons including tight protection and civil unrest in neighbouring countries.Chobe National Park, located in northern Botswana, is particularly renowned for its large elephant population. The park boasts an estimated 50,000 elephants, making it a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts. Visitors to Chobe National Park can enjoy game drives and boat cruises to witness these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat.Botswana's elephants play a crucial role in attracting tourists from around the world. Wildlife enthusiasts visit the country to witness these majestic creatures in their natural habitats. Elephant tourism generates revenue, which can be invested in conservation initiatives, local communities, and the overall development of the country

LUXURY SAFARI CRUISE ON THE CHOBE RIVER IN BOTSWANA

Boat cruising in Botswana is a popular activity, particularly in the northern regions of the country where there are rivers, deltas, and wetland areas. The most renowned destination for boat cruises in Botswana is the Okavango Delta, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of Africa's most spectacular inland water systems.

The Okavango Delta offers a unique opportunity to explore the pristine waterways, channels, and lagoons aboard a traditional mokoro (dugout canoe) or a motorized boat. These boat cruises allow visitors to experience the delta's breathtaking scenery, diverse wildlife, and serene atmosphere.

Lions in Botswana

Lions (Panthera leo) are one of the iconic and majestic animals found in Botswana. They are a prominent part of the country's diverse wildlife and can be spotted in various national parks and wildlife reserves.

Cape buffalo (Syncerus caffer)

In Botswana, the African buffalo, also known as the Cape buffalo (Syncerus caffer), is the species of buffalo that can be found. The African buffalo is one of the iconic "Big Five" animals and is known for its impressive size, strength, and herding behavior.

Leopards (Panthera pardus)

Leopards (Panthera pardus) are indeed found in Botswana. They are one of the most elusive and sought-after big cat species in the country. Leopards have a wide distribution across Africa, including various habitats in Botswana, ranging from arid regions to woodland and riverine areas.

Hippopotamus amphibius

Hippos (Hippopotamus amphibius) can be found in Botswana, particularly in the northern parts of the country where there are suitable waterways and wetland habitats.

Giraffes (Giraffa camelopardalis)

Giraffes are known for their long necks, unique patterns of spots, and gracefully towering presence. They inhabit various habitats, including woodlands, savannas, and open grasslands.
